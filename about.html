Alamadhi Semen Station

Alamadhi Semen Station was established on 1st May 2015 at Edapalayam, Red Hills, Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Alamadhi Semen Station is a unit of NDDB Dairy Services. NDDB Dairy Services is a not-for-profit Company under section 25 of the Companies Act and is a wholly-owned subsidiary of the NDDB.

It functions as a delivery arm of the NDDB for field operations relating to promoting Producer Companies and Productivity Services and is providing technical and management services by professionals drawn from the cooperative, private and developmental sectors..

Alamadhi Semen Station ranked second in India, consecutively for the last two Central Monitoring Unit evaluation, by Govt. of India. We are among the 4 mega semen stations owned or managed by NDDB Dairy Services which produces Bovine frozen semen doses under the brand name "Superior Animal Genetics" (SAGTM)which is the largest selling Bovine semen brand in the country. Using advanced Genetics and strict adherence to Minimum Standard Protocols as enshrined by Govt. Of India. Over the last few decades, Artificial Insemination (AI) has been instrumental in increasing milk yield and thereby doubling the farmer's income in the country. One of the signature benefits of AI is that this method has the potential for rapid dissemination of elite male genetic material over a large geographical area. Even though AI is a very powerful and useful technique for increasing the productivity of indigenous animals, the backbone of an effective AI program is the quality of the semen available. As more and more farmers are adopting AI the demand for quality semen has grown exponentially in the country. This has led to the advent of very high-priced semen as well as duplicate semen from various spurious sources.

In light of this NDDB Dairy Services, with its deep-rooted commitment to providing affordable high-quality genetics to the dairy farmers, Alamadhi Semen station has been commissioned at Red Hills, Chennai, Tamil Nadu on 1st May 2015. The semen produced from this semen station is marketed under the brand Superior Animal Genetics (SAGTM).

What sets the brand SAGTM apart from the rest:

  • Best genetics at affordable rates
  • Unwavering commitment to quality
  • Constant strive to improve and bring the latest technology
  • Quality Assurance to our customers

These factors have helped us become the top-selling and also the most revered semen brand in the country. The semen stations under the brand SAG maintain more than 2000 high pedigreed bulls of various breeds. SAG is the only brand offering a great variety of indigenous cattle and buffaloes.
